Transcribe an online meeting (Zoom / Meet / Teams)

  1. Join your meeting in another browser tab (use the web version of Zoom/Meet/Teams).
  2. Click Start capture below → in the picker choose that tab and enable “Also share tab audio”.
  3. Live captions appear while the meeting runs. When you Stop, you get a full transcript with speaker labels and an AI summary.
